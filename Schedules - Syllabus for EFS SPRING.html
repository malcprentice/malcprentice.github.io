General Description

English for Science I and II will help students develop reading, academic writing, listening, speaking and vocabulary skills using topics related to their majors. Students will also learn basic research skills. For Second-year International Technology Cooperation Education Program (ITCEP) students

Goals and Objectives

This course follows the Curriculum for General Academic Skills, with the addition of

  • write a sectional essay
  • outline an academic article

Required Materials

  • Materials provided by teacher
  • A4 40 pocket plastic folder

Projects and Presentations 10% Project A (Poster) No see weekly schedule
Projects and Presentations 20% Project B (Pres+Essay) No see weekly schedule
Projects and Presentations 10% Project C (Free) No see weekly schedule

Speed Reading (SR) 0% 0 max 20 extra = 5 OK points see do a speed reading

Participation points

See get independent learning points

However, there are special rules for EFS this year:

  • RR can be submitted for 1 participation point each. Need to score a minimum of C under rubric or you will have to do them again.
  • ALC NetAcademy is now REQUIRED - no extra points available.
  • TOEIC IP official test (not required, costs money) - 5 participation points instead of 2.

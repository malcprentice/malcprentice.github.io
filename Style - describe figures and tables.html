Interpretation phrases

Look at Index - Style index - after you describe the graph, you can

  • write a cause-effect sentence about what it means
  • write a compare-contrast sentence to compare it to previous research or another figure
  • write a partition sentence to separate multiple features in the table/figure
  • use hedging to cast doubt on the reliability of the data
  • Summarize the graph
    • see use cohesion for summary language

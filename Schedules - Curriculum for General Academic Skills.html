Skills with no mark are covered in every course.

Skills marked * are covered second year and advanced courses only.

By the end of this course you will be able to:

express your ideas clearly (Style)

By the end of this course you will be able to:

  • follow academic conventions - Academic numbers, avoiding "I", etc
  • use cohesion to join your ideas
  • lead and participate in discussions
  • Write sentences to match your Arrangement:
    • write a definition sentence
    • write a partition sentence
    • write a cause-effect sentence
    • write a compare-contrast sentence
  • Avoid common writing problems
    • avoid repeating words
    • avoid short sentences
  • Avoid common grammar problems
    • use a parenthetical statement
    • use gerunds and infinitives
  • Express yourself clearly in discussion:
    • express opinions, agree, and disagree
  • Other necessary English, as needed

    • use hedging*
    • use keywords
    • use lists
    • use the right tense
    • use title case

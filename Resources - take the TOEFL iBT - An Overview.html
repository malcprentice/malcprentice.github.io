Overview

Reading 60-80 mins 3 academic texts (maybe more) take the TOEFL iBT - 1 - Reading
Listening 60-90 mins 4 lectures, 2 conversations (maybe more) take the TOEFL iBT - 2 - Listening
10m break
Independent Speaking 1min X 2 Free choice + Paired Choice take the TOEFL iBT - 3 - Independent speaking
Integrated Speaking 20min=90s X 4 Read-Listen-Speak & Listen-Speak (Campus/Lecture) take the TOEFL iBT - 4 - Integrated speaking
Integrated Writing 20 mins Read-Listen-Write take the TOEFL iBT - 5 - Integrated writing
Independent Writing 30 mins Write take the TOEFL iBT - 6 - Independent writing

  • (maybe more) = you may get an extra set of readings or listening
    • (4 readings, or 6 lectures/3 conversations).
    • This is TOEFL checking their materials. One of the sets is not included in your grade.

General tips

  • Don't forget your ID with signature and name
  • You are expected to take notes.
    • They will give you paper
    • It will be destroyed after the test.
  • Do NOT listen to the directions.
    • You should already know what you are doing.
    • Click CONTINUE and spend your time on the questions/content.
  • Get some sleep
  • Check your score breakdown, and work on your weakness
  • Take the test several times - the test centres have lots of little booths, and it's not like practising at home in a quiet room.

Tips for all writing questions

  • Learn to type fast
    • 10 wpm = 150 word independent essay in 20 mins, with 10 mins thinking time.
    • With practice, you can easily do 30wpm.
    • 30wpm = 150 word independent essay in 5 mins, with 25 mins thinking time.
    • Practice typing. See do a quickwrite
  • TOEFL test are run using US keyboards.
    • Some keys (@ / â€œ) are in different places.
    • The space bar is bigger
    • A US keyboard (USB) for home practice would cost about 700 yen.
  • In practice, make a list of grammar/spelling mistakes you often make, and check for them

What score do I need?

Soka University Exchange program 79
Average US undergraduate degree 74 (60-100 depending on University)
Average US graduate degree 83 (But expect 100+ for a good university)
UK degrees TOEFL iBT not accepted. Take IELTS (link)

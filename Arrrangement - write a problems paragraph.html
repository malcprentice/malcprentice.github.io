Basic arrangement and style

This paragraph should have 12 sentences minimum

Each sentence should be 10 words absolute minimum, but 15 is better.

This is the simple version - just copy and change.

For more advanced options, see the bottom

  1. Problems paragraph P
  2. Sentence 1: "This paragraph looks at three ways in which production of X causes problems - KEYWORD1, KEYWORD2, and KEYWORD3."
  3. Sentence 2: Cohesion "The first problem is KEYWORD"
  4. Sentence 3: Detail sentence of KEYWORD 1 (APA reference)
  5. Sentence 4: LINK WORD + second detail sentence of KEYWORD 1
  6. Sentence 5: Cohesion "The second problem is KEYWORD 2"
  7. Sentence 6: Detail sentence of KEYWORD 2
  8. Sentence 7: LINK WORD + second detail sentence of KEYWORD 2
  9. Sentence 8: Cohesion "The third problem is KEYWORD 3"
  10. Sentence 9: Detail sentence of KEYWORD 3
  11. Sentence 10: LINK WORD + second detail sentence sentences of KEYWORD 3
  12. Sentence 11: Summary ("In summary, there are three main problems - KEYWORD1, KEYWORD2, and KEYWORD3 - of which the most serious is [CHOOSE ONE KEYWORD]")
  13. Sentence 12: Link to solutions paragraph ("However, the next paragraph examines some possible solutions to these issues.")

What is a KEYWORD?

  • Simple answer: use only noun or short noun phrase
  • Advanced options: use keywords

What is a "LINK WORD"

  • "For example" or "However" or "In addition"
  • See use cohesion

Advanced options

1) Add up to 3 more sentences

If you have more to say, you could add one more detail sentence to each keyword, for a maximum of 15 sentences. Any more and it will start getting too long.
